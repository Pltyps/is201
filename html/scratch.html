I Love playing the Pokémon Video Games.
Below are the top 5 Pokémon games ranked based on my gameplay experiences:

  1. Pokémon HeartGold Version & Pokémon SoulSilver Version
    • This Generation 4 games is a remake of the Generation 2 Pokémon Gold and Silver Games, while utilizing the improvements implemented in Pokémon Crystal. It also implements the Generation 1 games as post-game content, making this game worth your money and time.

    Image courtesy of The Pokémon Company
  2. Pokémon Legends: Arceus
    • This Generation 8 game is the most unique entry to the mainline Pokémon games and also serves as a prequel to the Generation 4 Pokémon Diamond, Pearl and Platinum Versions. Though the graphical fideltiy is a little rough, the unique gameplay and compelling storyline makes it a must-play game despite the short runtime.

    Image courtesy of The Pokémon Company
  3. Pokémon Emerald Version
    • This Generation 3 game is a director's cut version of the other Generation 3 Pokémon games namely Pokémon Ruby and Pokémon Saphhire. This game improves on the groundwork laid by the other two games whilst updating the character sprites with various animations. It also added a post-game area, the Battle Frontier which employed various game mechanics to add a greater variety of battles and hours more of enjoyment and sense of accomplishments.

    Image courtesy of The Pokémon Company
  4. Pokémon Platinum Version
    • This Generation 4 game is another director's cut version of the other Generation 4 Pokémon games namely Pokémon Diamond and Pokémon Pearl. This game also improves on the groundwork laid by the other two games whilst shifting focus on the mysterious third legendary Pokémon resulting in a unique encounter experience never before seen, and never seen since. It also improves on and fixes issues that players had with Pokémon Diamond and Pokémon Pearl making it the best option of the 3 to pick up and play.

    Image courtesy of The Pokémon Company
  5. Pokémon Black Version & Pokémon White Version
    • This Generation 4 game is another director's cut version of the other Generation 4 Pokémon games namely Pokémon Diamond and Pokémon Pearl. This game also improves on the groundwork laid by the other two games whilst shifting focus on the mysterious third legendary Pokémon resulting in a unique encounter experience never before seen, and never seen since. It also improves on and fixes issues that players had with Pokémon Diamond and Pokémon Pearl making it the best option of the 3 to pick up and play.
